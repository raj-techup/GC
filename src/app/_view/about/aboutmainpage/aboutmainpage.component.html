Welcome To Greencrosslab

From it s humble beginning from a small 200 sq ft laboratory in paldi, greencross laboratory has now reached to almost 20000 sq ft area in all over Gujarat. Greencross has 17 branches in Gujarat and it is leading laboratory in Gujarat.

Accreditation and Quality Standards

Greencross Laboratory, accredited by NABL since 2007, maintains rigorous quality standards, ensuring accurate and reliable diagnostic results. This accreditation signifies adherence to international practices, instilling confidence in patients and healthcare providers.

Extensive Coverage and Accessibility

Greencross Lab offers round-the-clock services across Gujarat, Madhya Pradesh, and Rajasthan, with full-working laboratories strategically located in key cities. This extensive coverage ensures accessibility and convenience for patients seeking medical testing.

Leadership in Technology and Expertise

Description: Greencross Lab leads in technology and expertise, boasting the largest number of pathologists and qualified personnel in Gujarat. Since 1980, it has been at the forefront of introducing new tests and technologies, emphasizing innovation and superior service in laboratory medicine.
