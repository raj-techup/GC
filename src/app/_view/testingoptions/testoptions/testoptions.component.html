  • LAB@HOME

    Undergoing a blood test can be a nervous and stressful experience for many. we help you by providing you service right at the comfort of your home. Our Home initiative is the ideal home laboratory solution for senior citizens, people with disabilities, people who prefer the privacy of their home and even people who are simply busy at work. Highly trained team of phlebotomists are experts in single prick blood collection. We understand your needs and hence we collect samples anytime of the day. You may either book your appointment online by filling a simple online form or just call our regional office.
